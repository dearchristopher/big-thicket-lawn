# Big Thicket Lawn Services - Website Redesign Proposal

## Design Philosophy: "Trust at First Glance"

The new design prioritizes **immediate value communication** and **trust establishment**. Within 5 seconds, visitors should understand:
1. What you do (lawn & landscape services)
2. What it costs (transparent pricing)
3. Why choose you (social proof + differentiation)
4. How to get started (clear CTAs)

---

## Color Palette (Preserved)

| Color | Usage |
|-------|-------|
| **Green 700/800/900** | Primary brand, headers, trust elements |
| **Yellow-400/500** | Accents, highlights, CTAs |
| **Red-600** | Urgency, important actions, emphasis |
| **Gray-50/100/200** | Backgrounds, cards, separation |
| **White** | Clean space, contrast |

---

## Typography (Preserved)

- **Headlines**: Bungee/Orbitron (decorative, bold)
- **Body**: Roboto Condensed (clean, readable)

## Key Design Decisions

### 1. **Pricing First Approach**
- Pricing appears in the hero (even if just "Starting at $55")
- Full transparent pricing section is the first major content block
- Each service card includes starting prices
- No "Contact us for pricing" - immediate transparency builds trust

### 2. **Social Proof Everywhere**
- Hero: Star rating + short testimonial
- Trust bar immediately below hero
- Before/after gallery for visual proof
- Dedicated testimonials section
- Service area shows you're local

### 3. **Frictionless Conversion**
- Multiple "Get Quote" CTAs throughout
- Phone number always accessible
- YardBook integration preserved
- FAQ section reduces hesitation

### 4. **Mobile-First Thinking**
- Sticky header with quick-call button on mobile
- Pricing cards stack vertically
- Before/after slider touch-friendly
- Click-to-call throughout
